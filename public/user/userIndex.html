Welcome to Timekeeper!


Our planet is in danger! Hostile aliens want to invade Earth with many spaceships...

Fortunately, the spaceships are sent at regular intervals, and produce a short sound. A laser cannon shooting at the same regular intervals may hit and destroy them...

Only YOU can help us! You are a Timekeeper, gifted with extraordinary timing skills...

But be careful! After a certain time, the spaceships become invisible and do not produce any sounds... It is up to YOU to keep a good timing and continue to hit the invisible spaceships!

Synchronize your shots with the sounds, keep a steady rhythm, and save us all...
